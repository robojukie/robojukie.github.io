Volunteer

Roca Blanca: 2007 and 2008

Roca Blanca is a mission base located in Oaxaca, Mexico.

In 2007, traveled with a team of 6 to Roca Blana mission base in Mexico. We joined other members and traveled to a rural mountain village in Ixtapa, Oaxaca (not the same Ixtapa known for surfing. We provided health care and education about sanitary water. We also provided activities for the children of the village.

In 2008, traveled with a team of mostly college students to Mexico and visited multiple rural cities in Oaxaca. We primarily provided dental care.

Camp Barnabas: 2004, 2005, and 2006

Camp Barnabas is a camp for disabled children and adults. Different weeks target different disabilities, such as deafness, blindness, physical disabilities, and mental disabilities. Siblings of children may also attend.

In 2004, attended Camp Barnabas as a Barnrat/Barnstormer. Younger volunteers worked in the kitchen and restrooms to help camp operations.

In 2005 and 2006, served as a camp counselor. As a counselor, I was the primary caretaker of a disabled camper, responsible for the campers 24/7, including mobility, feeding, bathing, etc.
